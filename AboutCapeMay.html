What To Do In Cape May!

Summer Fall Winter Spring
Beach Relax on the beach and swim! Look at the wildlife swim in the cold water! Bring a hot chocolote and visit a snowey beach! Go for a hike on the beach and bird watch!
Town Get ice cream and go shopping on historic Washington Street Mall! Check out the many restaurants on the island like The Blue Pig or Exit Zero filling station! Attend the cape may Christmas paraide and look at the decorated houses! Visit historic Congress Hall! and get a massage!
Inland Visit Tree to Tree, a high ropes course that is is fun for the whole family! Participate in the Cape May Turkey Trot, an annual Thanksgiving 5k! Go to the various antique stores and find the next conversation statrer for your home! Visit Beach Plum Farm, an inland farm and look at the cute newborn animals!
